The Queen's Hotel

94 Dunlop St. E., Barrie

Previous Next
  • Phone: 705.728.2424
  • Address:
    94 Dunlop St. E., Barrie
  • Intersection: Dunlop St. / Mulcaster St.

For over 30 years the The Queen's Hotel has been a night-club and sports bar on Dunlop St. East near Mulcaster St. in downtown Barrie.

Open six days a week, The Queen's Hotel has been called "Barrie's favourite hometown bar where all the locals come to play!" Whether seeking lunch or dinner from the Italian-Canadian menu or late night entertainment, guests can stop by The Queens six days a week and enjoy a cool drink in comfortable surroundings.
